What is a Word Counter?

A word counter is a free online tool that counts the number of words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs in any text. It's essential for writers, students, SEO professionals, and anyone who needs to meet specific length requirements.

Common Uses

  • Checking essay and article length requirements
  • SEO meta description optimization (keep under 160 characters)
  • Social media post length (Twitter 280, Instagram captions)
  • Academic writing with strict word count limits
  • Translation billing by word count
