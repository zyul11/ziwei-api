Tian Ji (天機): The Secretary Star — Your Tech Insight Compass

Tian Ji, the Secretary Star, belongs to the Wood element and ranks as the third star of the Southern Dipper. Representing the legendary strategist Jiang Ziya, Tian Ji governs intelligence, planning, information processing, and technological innovation.

When Tian Ji occupies your Life Palace (Ming Gong) or Wealth Palace (Cai Bo Gong), you possess a natural sixth sense for emerging technologies. The 2026 annual transformation amplifies this: Tian Ji Hua Lu (Prosperity Transformation) means 2026 is a golden window for tech innovation to convert into real commercial value. Investors with strong Tian Ji placements in the Life, Wealth, or Career Palaces have a knack for identifying undervalued tech stocks before they surge.

Wu Qu (武曲): The Wealth Star — Turning Tech into Treasure

Wu Qu, the Wealth Star, belongs to the Metal element and is the sixth star of the Northern Dipper. It is the purest wealth star in all of Ziwei Doushu — governing not just money, but capital management, business instincts, and financial strategy.

When Wu Qu sits strongly (Miao or Wang) in your Wealth Palace — particularly in Zi, Wu, Shen, or Yin positions — you have exceptional wealth-building ability. You can convert abstract technical value into concrete financial returns. This is the exact mechanism behind the Hang Seng Tech Index’s AI additions: technological prowess must be amplified and monetized through capital markets.

Two key states of Wu Qu to watch:

  • Strong Wu Qu (Miao/Wang): Excellent financial instincts, picks winning tech stocks, thrives in volatile markets
  • Weakened Wu Qu (Xian/Xian): Inconsistent wealth luck — needs supporting auspicious stars like Tian Kui or Tian Yue to stabilize returns

The 2026 Po Jun Hua Lu (Destructor Prosperity Transformation) signals that old wealth models are being overturned. A new tech-wealth paradigm is rising — and the Hang Seng Tech Index adding AI giants is textbook evidence.

Tai Yin (太陰): The Moon Star — The Hidden Hand of Finance

Tai Yin, the Moon Star, belongs to the Water element and represents financial liquidity, cyclical markets, and hidden wealth forces. Often underestimated, Tai Yin plays a crucial role in investment success.

People with Tai Yin in the Wealth Palace excel at long-term value investing. They have an almost instinctive feel for market cycles — knowing when to accumulate and when to harvest. The 2026 Tai Yin Hua Ke (Fame Transformation) means that hidden value will gradually surface in financial markets. Genuinely strong technology companies like Zhipu AI and MiniMax will finally receive the market recognition they deserve.
